Characteristics of the best types of businesses:

* Recurring revenue
* High margins
* Software driven
* Scalable growth
* High-ticket price
* Low to no inventory
* Few employees
* Low capital expenditiures financed by profits not debt

For example, online media businesses like job boards, newsletters, and paid communities.
